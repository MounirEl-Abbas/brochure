Why Visit Europe?

With centuries of history, popular films and timeless literature, there is a profound allure to Europe's cities and cultures. It has been romaticized for ages and for good reason. Many only dream of turning the European vacation in their hearts into reality. We make it a reality for you.

Discover Gastronomy

Belgians love gastronomy. We love good food and invite you to taste our special beers and cheeses and obviously our chocolate, waffles, bonbons, endives, beef stew, speculaas and the like. And did we mention that our fries are simply legendary? Visit the Le Chou de Bruxelles for its famous steaks and fries. Then stop by Les Petits Oignons for its world class desserts. Enjoy drinks at Coup de Foudre.

The Ardennes

The Ardenees, situated in the south-east of Belgium, are rich in fauna and flora. with vast forests of broadleaf and fir, hills and fast-flowing rivers. Visitors can wander through the many quaint, historical villages nestled in the valleys where tranditions and folklore still live on, and where the region's arts and crafts can be thoroughly enjoyed. Whether it is the springtime or the dead of winter, the Ardennes are a natural wonder to behold and explore!
